## 🎯 Platinum Tier Key Achievements

### Distributed Architecture
1. **Cloud Component**: Always-on operation handling email triage, draft creation, and monitoring
2. **Local Component**: Sensitive operations, approvals, and final actions
3. **Vault Synchronization**: Git-based with security-aware filtering
4. **Claim-by-Move System**: Coordination preventing double-work between components

### Security-First Design
5. **Credential Isolation**: Sensitive data never synced between cloud and local
6. **Role-Based Access**: Clear separation of sensitive vs non-sensitive operations
7. **Approval Workflows**: Human-in-the-loop for all sensitive actions
8. **Audit Logging**: Comprehensive tracking of all operations

### Production-Ready Features
9. **Health Monitoring**: Continuous system health checks with alerts

11. **Scalable Architecture**: Modular design supporting growth
