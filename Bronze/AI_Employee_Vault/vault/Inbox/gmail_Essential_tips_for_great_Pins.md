The best ads on Pinterest are visually compelling, tell a good story and make people want to learn more. Here are three tips to create your most successful ad:

* Keep creative vertical with an aspect ratio of 2:3—like 1000x1500 pixels
* Use clear, straightforward copy
* Match creative with your landing page to keep the user experience consistent
