Hi everyone,

GPT image generation, first introduced in ChatGPT last month, is live on Poe! You can use it directly within GPT-4o (https://poe.com/GPT-4o), and it's the new default image model for subscribers when they use Assistant (https://poe.com/assistant).

One of the best image models to date, it excels at understanding prompts to match your vision, offers sharp and accurate text renderings (great for posters, cards, and memes), and is exceptional at restyling photos.

Unique features to try it with on Poe:
- '/repeat' generates multiple versions at once (up to 10)
- Send new requests even while the previous image is generating
- Combine it with a video model (e.g., Kling or Veo 2) to bring images to life

Poe Apps and bots:
- Make incredible image-based Poe Apps by using the standalone GPT-Image-1 bot with App Creator (https://poe.com/App-Creator) (no coding skills required)
- Put your own spin on existing Poe Apps through our new Remix feature (try your own take on a retro throwback: https://poe.com/Yearbookify)
- Use it as a base model for your prompt and server bots

To get started, try GPT-4o here (https://poe.com/GPT-4o) or on any platform where you use Poe. We're looking forward to seeing what you create!
