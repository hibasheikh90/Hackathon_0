## Body
Hi everyone,

We're excited to share that Poe now supports audio generation through ElevenLabs and Cartesia! These bots allow you to transform any text into natural-sounding speech, using over 100 unique voices in many different languages.

Audio generation introduces new ways to explore your interests on Poe, especially when combined with your favorite bot. Here are a few examples:

* Ask Claude 3.5 Sonnet to create a narration of a topic you like, then use the 'Speak' button to turn it into a short, personalized podcast
* Pair the 1920's Radioman voice with Web Search bot to turn top news stories into an entertaining and informative nostalgic radio broadcast
* Utilize a longer context bot like GPT-4o-128k to summarize a technical paper or PDF into an easy-to-understand overview
* Generate a whimsical story with Grok-beta and transform it into an immersive audiobook using a unique voice from Cartesia

You can further customize the audio by using the --voice and --language parameters, as well as selecting the 'Revoice' follow-up button to try a new voice.

We're excited for you to try these new bots, and we'd love to hear your feedback at feedback@poe.com. Stay informed on our latest updates and announcements by following us on X or joining our Discord server.

Thanks,
The Poe Team
